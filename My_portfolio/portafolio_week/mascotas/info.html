Adoption Process


At Zootopia, we want every furry friend to find the perfect home. These are the steps:


  • Application: Complete our online pre-adoption form.
  • Interview: Talk with our coordinators to discuss your lifestyle.
  • Visit: Meet your future companion at our facilities.
  • Follow-up: We formalize the contract and support you during the transition.
